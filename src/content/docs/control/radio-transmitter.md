The radio transmitter (TX) is the handheld controller that sends your stick inputs to the quad's receiver.

## Key Features

- **Gimbals** — The joysticks. Hall-effect gimbals are smoother and more durable than potentiometer-based ones.
- **Protocol support** — What receivers it can talk to (ELRS, Crossfire, FrSky, etc.)
- **Ergonomics** — You'll hold this for hours. Comfort matters.
- **Switch/button layout** — For arming, flight modes, and other functions.

## Popular Transmitters

- **RadioMaster** — Boxer, Zorro, TX16S. Multi-protocol, great value.
- **TBS Tango 2** — Compact, Crossfire built-in.
- **FrSky** — Taranis series. Long-standing in the hobby.

## Mode 1 vs. Mode 2

- **Mode 2 (most common)** — Throttle on left stick, pitch/roll on right.
- **Mode 1** — Throttle on right stick. Common in some regions.
