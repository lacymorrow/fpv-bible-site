The receiver (RX) is a small module mounted on your quad that receives control signals from your radio transmitter.

## Key Considerations

- **Protocol compatibility** — Must match your transmitter (ELRS, Crossfire, FrSky, etc.)
- **Antenna placement** — Keep antennas away from carbon fiber and electronics for best signal.
- **Size/weight** — Smaller is better for racing builds.
- **Telemetry support** — Most modern receivers support bidirectional telemetry.

## Mounting Tips

- Mount with double-sided tape or a rubber grommet for vibration isolation.
- Route antennas at 90° to each other for antenna diversity.
- Keep antennas away from the VTX antenna to avoid interference.
