Motors convert electrical energy into rotational force that spins your propellers.

## Motor Specs

- **Size (e.g., 2207)** — First two digits = stator diameter (22mm), last two = stator height (07mm). Bigger stator = more torque.
- **KV Rating** — RPM per volt. Higher KV = faster but draws more current. Lower KV = more torque, more efficient.
  - **4S builds:** 2300-2600KV typical
  - **6S builds:** 1700-1950KV typical
- **Bearings** — Quality bearings = smoother, longer-lasting motors.

## Common Sizes for 5" Quads

- **2207** — The standard all-rounder.
- **2306** — Wider stator, quick response. Popular for racing.
- **2208** — Taller stator, more torque. Good for heavier builds.

## Racing Motors

Racing motors prioritize low weight, high KV, and fast response time. They're typically smaller stator (2207 or 2306) with high KV values tuned for 4S or 6S packs.
