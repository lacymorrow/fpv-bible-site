For high-quality footage, mount a dedicated action camera on your quad.

## Popular Cameras

- **GoPro** — The standard. GoPro Hero series, or the lighter GoPro Bones designed for FPV.
- **Insta360 Go** — Tiny, lightweight. Great for micro builds.
- **Runcam Thumb** — Budget-friendly, lightweight, decent quality.
- **DJI Action** — Competitive with GoPro, good stabilization.
- **Naked GoPro** — A GoPro stripped of its case and screen to save weight. Popular mod.

## Mounting

- Top-mount is standard (on top of the frame, tilted forward).
- Use TPU-printed mounts for vibration dampening.
- Secure with a strap or mount clips.
- Balance the camera position — too far forward or back affects flight characteristics.

## Stabilization

- **ReelSteady / Gyroflow** — Software stabilization using gyro data from the action camera or flight controller. Produces incredibly smooth footage from raw shaky video.
