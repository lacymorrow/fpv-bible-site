DVR records the video feed as displayed in your goggles — OSD, static, and all.

## Why DVR?

- Review crashes to understand what went wrong.
- Analyze your racing lines.
- Share raw FPV footage with the community.
- Record evidence if something goes wrong (flyaway, interference).

## Quality

- Analog DVR is low resolution (typically 640x480 or similar).
- Digital DVR (DJI, HDZero) records at much higher quality.
- Not suitable for high-production video — that's what an action camera is for.

## Most goggles have built-in DVR. Just make sure you have a microSD card inserted and formatted.
