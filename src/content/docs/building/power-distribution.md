The power distribution step connects your battery connector to the PDB or AIO flight controller, providing power to every component.

## Steps

1. Solder the XT60 pigtail (battery lead) to the main power pads.
2. Add a capacitor across the main power pads (reduces voltage spikes and video noise).
3. Tin all ESC pads before mounting.
4. Verify polarity before connecting a battery.

## Tips

- Use at least the same wire gauge as your battery lead for power connections.
- A low-ESR capacitor (1000uF 35V) across the main power pads is cheap insurance against video noise and brownouts.
