ESCs mount between the PDB/FC stack and the motors. For a 4-in-1 ESC, it's part of your stack. For individual ESCs, they mount on the arms.

## 4-in-1 ESC

1. Stack on standoffs above or below the flight controller.
2. Solder motor wires to the labeled pads (M1-M4).
3. Connect to FC via ribbon cable or solder pads.

## Individual ESCs

1. Shrink-wrap each ESC.
2. Mount on frame arms with double-sided tape or zip ties.
3. Solder power leads to PDB.
4. Solder signal wire to FC.
5. Solder 3 motor wires to each ESC.
