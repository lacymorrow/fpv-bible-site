## Wiring

1. Solder to a spare UART on the FC (TX, RX, 5V, GND).
2. Configure the UART in Betaflight for your protocol (CRSF, SBUS, etc.).
3. Bind to your transmitter.

## Antenna Placement

- Route antennas away from carbon fiber (blocks signal).
- Mount antennas at 90° to each other for diversity.
- Use antenna tubes (zip-tied to the frame) to protect and position antennas.
- Keep away from the VTX antenna.

## Binding

Each protocol has its own binding procedure. Consult your receiver's documentation.
