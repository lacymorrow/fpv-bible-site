## Mounting

1. Use the screws that came with your motors (usually M3).
2. Don't over-tighten — you can strip the motor mount threads.
3. Loctite (blue/medium) on motor screws prevents them from vibrating loose.

## Motor Direction

- Betaflight default: motors 1 & 4 spin clockwise, motors 2 & 3 counter-clockwise ("props in").
- Swap any two of the three motor wires to reverse direction.
- Or reverse in BLHeli configurator / Betaflight motor direction settings.

## Wire Routing

- Route motor wires cleanly along the arms.
- Secure with zip ties or electrical tape.
- Leave enough slack for arm flex but not so much that wires can contact props.
