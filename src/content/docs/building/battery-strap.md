The battery strap holds your LiPo in place during aggressive maneuvers. A battery ejection mid-flight is a bad day.

## Battery Strap

- Use a quality non-slip strap (rubberized or silicone-grip).
- Route through the frame's battery slot.
- Tighten firmly but don't crush the battery.

## Battery Pad / Protector

- Stick a grip pad or anti-slip mat on top of the frame where the battery sits.
- Protects the battery from vibration and the frame's carbon fiber edges.
- Some pilots add a thin foam pad underneath for extra protection.
