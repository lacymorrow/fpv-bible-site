Carbon fiber frames are CNC-cut, and the edges can be sharp and rough. Sanding them smooth is the first step in a clean build.

## Why Sand?

- **Removes sharp edges** that can cut wires and fingers.
- **Prevents delamination** at stress points.
- **Better adhesion** for tape and mounting.

## How To

1. Use 220-400 grit sandpaper.
2. Lightly sand all cut edges until smooth.
3. Wipe clean with a damp cloth to remove carbon dust.
4. **Wear a mask** — carbon fiber dust is not something you want to breathe.
