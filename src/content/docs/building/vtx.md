## Wiring

1. Connect to the FC's VTX pads (video signal in, 5V or VBAT power, GND).
2. If using Smart Audio or IRC Tramp, connect the control wire to a spare UART TX pad.
3. Configure in Betaflight OSD or VTX tables.

## Mounting

- Mount in the stack or on top of the stack.
- Ensure good airflow — VTXs get hot, especially at higher power.
- Route the antenna pigtail out the back of the frame.

## Antenna

- **Never power on without an antenna** — will burn out the VTX.
- Use a quality MMCX/UFL to SMA pigtail.
- Mount the antenna where it has a clear view of the sky.
