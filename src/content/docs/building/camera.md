## Mounting

1. Slide the camera into the frame's camera mount.
2. Secure with the included screws or side plates.
3. Set the tilt angle (racing: 30-45°, freestyle: 20-35°).

## Wiring

- **Analog:** Video out → VTX video in. Power from 5V or regulated voltage pad.
- **Digital:** Single cable to the digital VTX unit (varies by system).

## Settings

- Set aspect ratio (4:3 recommended for FPV).
- Adjust WDR/dynamic range for your flying environment.
- Most settings are accessible via the OSD or a joystick on the camera.
