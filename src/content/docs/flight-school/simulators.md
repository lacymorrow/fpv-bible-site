Simulators let you practice flying with your actual radio transmitter, in a virtual environment, with zero risk.

## Why Simulate?

- Free crashes — no repair bills.
- Fly anytime, anywhere — weather doesn't matter.
- Build muscle memory before risking real hardware.
- Try maneuvers you'd never attempt with a real quad.

## Popular Simulators

- **Liftoff** — Realistic physics, active community, Steam.
- **Velocidrone** — Considered the most realistic by many competitive pilots.
- **Uncrashed** — Modern, great visuals, Unreal Engine.
- **DRL Simulator** — Drone Racing League's official sim.
- **FPV Freerider** — Simple, cheap, good for absolute beginners.

## Setup

1. Connect your radio transmitter to your computer via USB.
2. Select the correct controller in the simulator settings.
3. Calibrate sticks and set your preferred rates.
4. Start with hover practice, then circuits, then acro maneuvers.
