You've built a quad, practiced on the sim, and you're ready to fly for real.

## Preflight Checklist

- All screws tight (especially motor and prop screws)
- Props on correct motors (CW/CCW matched)
- Battery secured and balanced
- Failsafe configured and tested
- VTX on an open channel
- Antennas connected (both radio and video)
- Flying area clear of people and obstacles

## First Hover

1. Find a large, open field. No trees, no people, no wind (ideally).
2. Arm the quad.
3. Slowly raise throttle until it lifts off.
4. Hover at eye level. Get a feel for the controls.
5. Practice maintaining position.

## Next Steps

- Forward/backward, left/right movement
- Yaw (rotation) while hovering
- Simple circuits (fly in a square or figure-8)
- Gradually increase speed and altitude
- Start attempting basic acro maneuvers when comfortable
