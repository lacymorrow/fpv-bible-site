Beyond the quad itself, there's gear that makes flying more comfortable and organized.

## Field Gear

- **Bag / Backpack** — A dedicated FPV backpack keeps your quads, batteries, goggles, and transmitter organized and protected. Look for padded compartments and battery-safe storage.
- **Chair** — You'll be standing or sitting in a field for hours. A portable camping chair is a quality-of-life upgrade.
- **Shade** — A popup canopy or umbrella. Goggles in direct sunlight = sweat and reduced visibility.

## Racing Accessories

- **Flag** — For marking your pilot station at events.
- **Gate** — Practice gates for training. Inflatable or PVC pipe builds.
- **Ground Station** — A portable setup with a monitor, diversity receiver, and antennas. Used by spotters or for spectating.

## Maintenance

- **Spare props** — Always carry extras. You will break props.
- **Spare screws** — M3 and M2 in various lengths.
- **Field soldering kit** — Portable iron, solder, flux for emergency repairs.
- **Prop removal tool** — Makes swapping props faster.
