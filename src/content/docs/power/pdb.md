The Power Distribution Board (PDB) takes power from the battery and distributes it to the ESCs, flight controller, and other components.

## What a PDB Does

- Splits the main battery power to 4+ ESC pads
- Provides regulated voltage outputs (typically 5V and 12V) for the FC, VTX, and camera
- Some include current sensors for battery telemetry

## Modern Alternatives

Many modern flight controllers have PDBs built in, eliminating the need for a separate board. All-in-one (AIO) flight controllers often include the PDB, OSD, and sometimes even ESCs on a single board.
