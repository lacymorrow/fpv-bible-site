How you wire batteries together affects voltage and capacity.

## Series

Batteries in series **add voltage** while capacity stays the same. This is how LiPo cells are arranged inside a single pack — a 4S battery is four 3.7V cells in series = 14.8V nominal.

## Parallel

Batteries in parallel **add capacity** while voltage stays the same. Sometimes used to combine two smaller packs for longer flight times.

## In Practice

Most FPV pilots use a single LiPo pack (4S or 6S) and don't wire batteries together. The series/parallel concept is most relevant for understanding cell count and battery specs.
