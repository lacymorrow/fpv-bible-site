Proper charging is critical for battery health and safety.

## Charging Basics

- **Balance charge** — Always balance charge. This ensures all cells reach the same voltage.
- **1C charge rate** — Charge at 1x the battery's capacity (e.g., 1.3A for a 1300mAh pack) unless the battery is rated for higher.
- **Never leave unattended** — LiPo fires are real. Always monitor charging.
- **LiPo bag or ammo can** — Charge inside a fireproof container.

## Storage Charging

If you won't fly for more than a few days, storage charge your packs to ~3.8V per cell. Most chargers have a storage mode.

## Charger Types

- **Simple balance chargers** — Cheap, slow, one pack at a time.
- **Multi-port chargers** — Charge multiple packs simultaneously. Worth the investment.
- **Field chargers** — Portable chargers that run off a car battery or generator.
