LEDs serve both practical and aesthetic purposes on an FPV quad.

## Why LEDs?

- **Orientation** — Know which direction your quad is facing at a distance.
- **Regulations** — Some locations require lights for visibility.
- **Style** — Make your quad stand out at events and meetups.
- **Status** — Some FC firmware uses LEDs to indicate arming status, GPS lock, and warnings.

## Types

- **LED strips** — Flexible strips that can be cut to length. WS2812B is common (individually addressable RGB).
- **Single LEDs** — Bright indicator LEDs for front/rear orientation.
- **Motor LEDs** — Mount under motors for underglow effects.
