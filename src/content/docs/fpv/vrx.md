The Video Receiver (VRX) picks up the signal broadcast by the VTX and feeds it to your goggles or monitor.

## Built-in vs. External

- **Built-in** — Most goggles have a built-in VRX module. Convenient but limited by what the goggle supports.
- **External module** — Plugs into goggles with a module bay (e.g., Fatshark, Skyzone). Lets you upgrade the receiver without replacing goggles.

## Key Features

- **Diversity** — Uses two antennas and switches to the stronger signal. Dramatically improves reception.
- **Rapid channel scanning** — Quickly find active pilots on the band.
- **OLED display** — Some modules show channel, RSSI, and battery info.

## Popular Modules

- **RapidFIRE** — ImmersionRC's diversity module. Gold standard for analog.
- **TBS Fusion** — Another top analog diversity module.
- **Built-in digital** — DJI, HDZero, Walksnail goggles have integrated receivers.
