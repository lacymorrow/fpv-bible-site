The FPV camera is mounted on the front of your quad and captures the live video feed. It's not the same as an action camera for recording — the FPV camera is optimized for low latency and dynamic range, not resolution.

## Key Specs

- **Sensor size** — Micro (19mm) and Nano (14mm) are common. Full-size for some builds.
- **Aspect ratio** — 4:3 (more vertical view) or 16:9 (widescreen). 4:3 is preferred by most pilots.
- **Light handling** — Wide Dynamic Range (WDR) helps when flying in and out of shadows.
- **Latency** — Lower is better. Every millisecond counts at speed.

## Analog vs. Digital

- **Analog cameras** — Output a composite video signal to analog VTX. Cheap, low latency.
- **Digital cameras** — Part of a digital system (DJI, HDZero, Walksnail). HD quality but system-locked.

## Camera Angle

Tilt angle affects how your quad flies. More tilt = faster forward flight at lower throttle. Racing: 30-45°. Freestyle: 20-35°. Cruising: 10-20°.
