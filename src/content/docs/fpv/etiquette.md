FPV frequencies are a shared resource. Poor etiquette can ruin someone's flight or cause a crash.

## The Basics

- **Announce your frequency/channel before powering on your VTX.** Stepping on someone's channel mid-flight can cause a crash.
- **Use a frequency chart** — At events, coordinate channels so nobody overlaps.
- **Don't power on your VTX on the bench** — Wait until you're on the flight line and cleared.
- **Call out when you're arming and disarming.**
- **Fly within your skill level** — Don't endanger other pilots or their quads.
- **Retrieve your crashed quad promptly** — Don't leave debris on the course.

## At Events

- Follow the race director's instructions.
- Use assigned channels only.
- Be ready when it's your turn — don't hold up the rotation.
