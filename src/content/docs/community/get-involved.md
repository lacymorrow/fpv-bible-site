FPV is more fun with other people. Here's how to connect with the community.

## Online

- **Reddit** — r/fpv, r/Multicopter, r/multicopterbuilds
- **Discord** — Numerous FPV Discord servers for advice and community.
- **YouTube** — Follow pilots like Joshua Bardwell, Mr Steele, Rotor Riot for tutorials and inspiration.
- **Facebook Groups** — Local and global FPV groups for buying/selling and meetup coordination.
- **RotorBuilds** — Share your build specs and see what others are flying.

## In Person

- **Meetups** — Search for local FPV groups in your area. Most cities have regular fly days.
- **Racing Leagues** — MultiGP is the largest FPV racing league. Find a local chapter.
- **Events** — FPV festivals, races, and freestyle comps happen worldwide.

## Flight Schools

- [Flymore Academy](http://flymore.academy) — Online and in-person flight training.
